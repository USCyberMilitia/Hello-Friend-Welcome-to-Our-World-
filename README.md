# Projects

## BlindSite: Investigation Vault & Forensic Browser

**BlindSite** is an open-source forensic browser and encrypted evidence vault for high-risk digital investigations.

It is built for situations where simply visiting a site, viewing media, or manually saving content could create legal, psychological, operational, or evidentiary risk.

BlindSite can:

- capture modern web pages
- support clear web and Tor/darknet workflows
- block dangerous media from the investigator’s screen
- preserve images, video, and audio as encrypted evidence
- create sealed evidence packages for authorized reviewers
- support safer handoff to attorneys, investigators, nonprofits, agencies, or law enforcement
- maintain audit trails and custody records
- render saved pages without unsafe remote callbacks

Most tools force investigators into a bad choice:

> See the content to preserve it, or block it and lose it.

BlindSite creates a third option:

> **Block it from the user, preserve it encrypted, and reveal it only to authorized reviewers later.**

BlindSite is not trying to be a clone of any existing tool. It builds on the broader investigative web-capture category for a different mission: high-risk evidence containment, restricted custody, sealed export, blocked-media preservation, and safer review.

## Watchman: Police & Government Accountability Platform

Our accountability platform is designed to make government conduct searchable, reviewable, and publicly accountable.

The system is built to organize and display:

- officer profiles
- body camera footage
- use-of-force records
- internal affairs records
- civilian complaints
- training records
- disciplinary history
- public records data
- agency profiles
- case files
- video evidence
- searchable misconduct and accountability patterns

The goal is not harassment.

The goal is transparency.

People should be able to understand how power is being used, who is using it, and whether patterns of harm are being ignored.

This work began with police accountability(we have over 150 use of force videos and 740 police officers on our site), but now we have over 700 agencies available for search and review.

## Open A.I.R.

**Open A.I.R.(Open Annual Incarceration Review)** is a platform for incarcerated people, formerly incarcerated people, people under supervision, and affected families to share their experiences with the criminal legal system.

It is designed to support:

- prisoner grievances
- parole officer reviews
- prison staff accountability
- case narratives
- retrials every year(or other arbitrary period)
- audio, video, and written testimony
- public-interest review of correctional systems
- pattern detection across agencies and officials

Open A.I.R. exists because people inside systems often have the least ability to be heard.

We want to help change that.

## Redacta Pro

**Redacta Pro** is a document and audio and video redaction and evidence-preparation tool designed for high-volume records work.
It can do 129 hours of government quoted work in less than 5 minutes. And that's just the document side!

It is built for situations where people need to redact, process, organize, and prepare large volumes of sensitive documents quickly and safely.

It supports the broader mission of public records work, investigations, evidence review, legal preparation, and accountability research.

It will be released soon and has the following features:

- face detection
- selective redaction
- location/setting based redaction
- subtitles
- physical object redaction(soon to be feature)
- phrase-based audio redaction
- safer public release
- faster evidence review
- police accountability workflows
- privacy protection for uninvolved people

Body camera footage can expose truth, but it can also expose victims, witnesses, children, medical crises, and private moments.

Good accountability tools must protect both transparency and human dignity. This tool if implemented by just a relative handful of agencies will save tax payers millions of dollars a year and tens/potentially hundreds of thousand in government hours. We offer this tool with free onboarding and lifetime support to any government agency who is interested.
